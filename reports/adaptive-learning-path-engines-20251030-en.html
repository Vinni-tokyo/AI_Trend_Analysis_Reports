Key Terminology

Adaptive Learning Path Engine: Technology that analyzes learner performance, strengths, and improvement areas with AI, adapting content and activities in real time. Automatically generates personalized learning pathways.
LXP (Learning Experience Platform): Platform that uses machine learning algorithms to analyze learner profiles, behaviors, and skill gaps, automatically recommending relevant content and learning paths.
Constraint-Based Modeling: AI technology that identifies misconceptions and knowledge gaps from learner responses, providing personalized feedback and remediation. Adopted by Smart Sparrow.
Competency-Based Education: Educational model enabling learners to master specific skills at their own pace. Based on proficiency rather than completion time.
Four-Dimensional Personalized Learning: Area9 Lyceum Rhapsode's approach to personalization across four dimensions: learning content, pace, style, and mastery.
