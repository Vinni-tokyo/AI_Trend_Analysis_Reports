Competitive Landscape & Market Positioning

Two Major AI Browser Categories

AI browsers are divided into two major categories: smart helpers that add AI features like chat and summarization where users still browse (Arc Max, Brave Leo, Microsoft Edge Copilot, ChatGPT Atlas), and AI agents that browse and work autonomously (Perplexity Comet, Strawberry Browser).

Key Terminology

AI Agentic Browser - A browser that autonomously navigates websites and completes tasks based on user instructions. Represents a paradigm shift from "users browse" to "AI browses."
Smart Helper Browser - A browser that adds AI features like chat, summarization, and translation, but users themselves still perform browsing, extending the traditional approach.
Multimodal AI - AI that can understand and process multiple data formats including text, images, voice, and video. Gemini is a representative example.
Web Grounding - AI functionality that performs real-time web searches to generate responses based on current information. Perplexity and Gemini excel at this.
Opt-In Design - Privacy protection design where AI features are off by default and users must explicitly activate them. The 2025 industry standard.
