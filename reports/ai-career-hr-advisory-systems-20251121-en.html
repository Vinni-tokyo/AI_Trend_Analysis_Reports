5. Skills Ontology-Based Learning Path Recommendations: Fuel50, ESCO, SARO

In 2025, self-evolving ontologies emerged that automatically update as the workforce learns new skills. Fuel50's skills ontology recommends customized learning based on what's missing in employee profiles and what's trending in future-fit roles.

Key Terminology

ATS (Applicant Tracking System): Software that companies use to automatically filter resumes during the recruitment process.
Skills Ontology: A structured knowledge framework connecting roles, skills, mastery levels, and industries, with self-evolving capabilities that auto-update as the workforce learns.
AI Career Advisor: An AI-driven platform that analyzes individual interests, abilities, and experiences to provide customized career paths and learning roadmaps.
HCM (Human Capital Management): Enterprise software that integrates HR functions including recruitment, payroll, performance management, and learning.
Transferable Skills: Universal abilities applicable across different jobs (e.g., project management, data analysis).
