5. Establishment of Copyright and Ethical Frameworks

Current Status: 2025 marks significant progress in legal and ethical frameworks for AI-generated content. The U.S. Copyright Office released Part 2 of its AI report on January 29, 2025, clarifying that AI-generated outputs qualify for copyright only if humans provide sufficient creative input. On May 9, it published a 108-page report examining whether unauthorized use of copyrighted materials to train AI systems is defensible as fair use.

Legal & Policy Developments

‚Ä¢ Copyright Protection: Purely AI-generated content (without human intervention) has no copyright in the U.S. and EU
‚Ä¢ Fair Use Debate: Examining the legality of copyrighted material use for AI training
‚Ä¢ Educational Exceptions: Some countries have special rules for educational AI use
‚Ä¢ Transparency Requirements: Movement toward mandatory disclosure of AI-generated content

‚Ä¢ Student Perception Shift: Tendency not to view AI use as inherently unethical

Key Terminology

Magic Studio - Collective term for Canva's AI feature suite. A platform integrating multiple AI tools including Magic Write (text generation), Magic Design (design generation), and Magic Animate (animation creation)
AI Avatars - Virtual human characters generated by AI technology. Can create videos with natural speech and expressions from text without filming actual people
Text-Based Video Editing - Method of editing videos by editing the transcript text rather than the video footage. Deleting text also deletes corresponding video segments, enabling intuitive editing
Filler Word Removal - Feature that automatically detects and deletes meaningless words like "um," "uh," "like." Cleans up lecture recordings and educational videos to improve viewing experience
Generative AI Content Copyright - Scope of copyright protection for AI-generated content. In the U.S., copyright is granted only when humans provide sufficient creative input (2025 Copyright Office position)
LMS Integration (Learning Management System Integration) - Feature that connects with learning management systems like Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle, allowing content created with AI tools to be directly placed in courses
Smart Content - Educational content that AI automatically customizes based on learner progress, comprehension, and learning style. Key technology for realizing personalized learning
