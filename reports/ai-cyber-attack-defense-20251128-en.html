Key Terminology

XDR (Extended Detection and Response) - A platform that integrates multiple security layers including endpoints, networks, and cloud to detect and respond to threats
Zero Trust - A security model based on "trust no one," verifying all access. A new approach replacing traditional perimeter-based defense
SIEM - A system that collects and analyzes security events and logs to detect threats. Accuracy improved through AI integration
RaaS (Ransomware as a Service) - A business model providing ransomware as a service. Enables low-skill criminals to participate in attacks
Deepfake - Fake audio/video created using AI. Increasingly abused in fraud schemes to bypass identity verification
