Key Terminology

Pure AI Tutors - Standalone AI tutors using general-purpose generative AI like ChatGPT and Claude for educational purposes. Available 24/7 at free or low cost without requiring dedicated EdTech platforms. Command 59%+ market share.
AI-Enhanced Platform - Services like Khan Academy Khanmigo and Duolingo Max that strategically integrate generative AI into existing education platforms. Goes beyond simple AI addition to redesign learning experiences as AI-native.
Traditional Homework Help - Chegg-type services that search and provide human-created answer databases. Declining due to generative AI's advantages in price, speed, and versatility.
Adaptive Learning - Educational approach that optimizes learning paths in real-time based on student comprehension and pace. Only achievable at scale through AI, projected to exceed $20B by 2027 with 40% CAGR.
Multimodal Learning - Learning experiences combining multiple formats including text, audio, video, and interactive content. AI algorithms select and provide optimal modalities based on learner styles.
LMS (Learning Management System) - Platforms used by educational institutions and enterprises for learning content delivery, progress tracking, and assessment. 47% planning AI migration as of 2025.
MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) - Large-scale online courses accessible to millions through platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udemy. Used by 20% of global learners in 2023.
