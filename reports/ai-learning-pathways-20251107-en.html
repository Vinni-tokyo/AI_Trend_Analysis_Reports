Key Terminology

Deep Reinforcement Learning - AI learning method through trial and error. Continuously adjusts optimal learning paths based on learners' real-time responses.
Knowledge Graph - Database structuring relationships between learning content. Defines learning dependencies such as "algebra required before calculus" to determine efficient learning sequences.
Skill Gap Analysis - Process identifying differences between current and target skill levels. Automated and visualized at individual and team levels through AI.
Adaptive Learning - Learning approach automatically adjusting content and difficulty based on learner comprehension and progress. Provides optimized learning experiences for each individual.
LMS (Learning Management System) - Platform for centralized management of online course delivery, progress tracking, and assessment.
Nanodegree - Short intensive online degree programs offered by Udacity. Acquire industry-aligned practical skills in 3-6 months.
ROI (Return on Investment) - Calculated by dividing training cost reduction or productivity gains from AI learning tool implementation by implementation costs.
