Key Terminology

Adaptive Learning - A learning method where AI adjusts difficulty and sequence of materials in real-time based on learner comprehension and pace. Research shows up to 30% improvement in learning outcomes.
Socratic Method - An educational technique that guides learners to derive answers themselves through questions rather than providing direct responses. Effective for developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
AI-Assisted Grading - A system that uses AI and natural language processing to automatically grade multiple-choice, short-answer, and programming assignments. Reduces teacher grading time by 40-50%.
Personalized Learning - Educational experiences customized to each learner's interests, abilities, and learning styles. AI has made large-scale personalization achievable.
NLP (Natural Language Processing) - Technology enabling computers to understand, generate, and process human language. Used in AI tutors and essay assessment systems for context understanding and appropriate feedback generation.
