Key Terminology

LMS (Learning Management System) - Platform for centralized management of online learning content delivery, student management, assessment, and progress tracking
LTI (Learning Tools Interoperability) - Standard specification for seamlessly connecting different educational tools with LMS
Socratic Method - Educational approach guiding learners to find solutions through questions rather than providing direct answers
Adaptive Learning - System where AI analyzes individual learner behavior to adjust learning content and difficulty in real-time
Predictive Analytics - Technology where AI predicts learning outcomes and risks from student participation, assignment submission, and assessment patterns
AI Answer Grouping - Feature where AI automatically classifies student responses to short-answer and multiple-choice questions, streamlining grading work
Personalized Learning Recommendation - AI system suggesting optimal courses and materials for each learner based on learning history and behavioral data
