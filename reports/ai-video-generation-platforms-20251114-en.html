Key Terminology

Synchronized Audio - Technology where AI simultaneously generates dialogue, background music, and ambient sound with the video, including lip-sync processing. Standardized by Veo 3 and Sora 2 in 2025.
Character Consistency - Technology maintaining identical character appearance, clothing, facial features, and body across multiple scenes. Runway Gen-4's reference image system is representative.
Multi-shot Consistency - Ability to maintain consistent characters and environments despite camera angle and lighting changes. Sora 2's core strength.
Previsualization (Previsualization) - Pre-production stage where scenes are visualized before filming. AI video generation enables cost reduction from $50k ‚Üí $14k.
TAKE IT DOWN Act - US legislation criminalizing unauthorized AI-generated intimate imagery, signed May 2025. Imposes 48-hour takedown obligation on platforms.
