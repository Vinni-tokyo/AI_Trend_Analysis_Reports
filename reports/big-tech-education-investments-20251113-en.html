Key Terminology

AI Literacy - The ability to understand AI technology fundamentals and effectively utilize AI tools in daily and professional contexts. Google and Microsoft's core investment focus
Generative AI in Education - Application of AI systems like ChatGPT and Copilot that generate text, images, and code in educational settings. Used for personalized learning, content creation, and automated feedback
Immersive Learning - Educational approach using VR/AR technology where students learn through direct experience in virtual environments. Meta Quest for Education is representative
EdTech Accelerator - Programs providing funding, mentorship, and technical infrastructure to education technology startups for accelerated growth. AWS Education Accelerator offers up to $100K credits
AI Tutoring Platform - Platforms providing AI-powered personalized learning experiences. Include Carnegie Learning, Duolingo, Squirrel AI, with $3.55B market size in 2025
