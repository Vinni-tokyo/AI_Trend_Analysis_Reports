Key Terminology

Digital Credentials/Open Badges - Verifiable digital certifications representing specific skills or achievements, compliant with Open Badges 3.0 standard (IMS Global/1EdTech). Examples: AWS Certified Solutions Architect badge, Google Analytics Certification.
Micro-Credentials - Small-scale, focused certifications proving mastery of specific skills rather than broad degrees. Preferred by 72% of employers for demonstrating job-ready competencies.
Skills-Based Hiring - Recruitment approach prioritizing demonstrated skills (verified via badges/credentials) over traditional 4-year degrees. Adopted by IBM, Google, Microsoft, Apple for tech roles.
ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) - Subscription revenue model where customers pay yearly fees. Credly uses enterprise ARR model ($996+/year) for predictable revenue streams.
LMS (Learning Management System) - Educational software platforms (Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard) managing courses and student data. Badgr integrates with LMS for seamless credential issuance.
