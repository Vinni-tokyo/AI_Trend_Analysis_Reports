Key Terminology

Unicorn Company - A privately-held startup valued at $1 billion or more. Called "unicorn" due to rarity
Mega-Round - Venture funding round of $100 million or more. Average mega-round for unicorns is approximately $300 million
LTV/CAC Ratio - Lifetime Value √∑ Customer Acquisition Cost. Ratio of 3:1 or higher is considered healthy
Hypergrowth - Annual growth of 2-3x (200-300%). Growth pace necessary to achieve unicorn status
LXP (Learning Experience Platform) - Learning platform with AI personalization, skill mapping, and curation capabilities beyond traditional LMS
TAM (Total Addressable Market) - Maximum market size that a product or service could theoretically reach
Micro-Credentials - Small-scale digital certifications proving acquisition of specific skills. Obtainable in shorter time than degrees
