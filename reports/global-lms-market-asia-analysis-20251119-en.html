Key Terminology

LMS (Learning Management System): Software platform that enables creation, delivery, and management of digital learning content. Used by educational institutions and corporations.
AI Adaptive Learning: Technology that uses artificial intelligence to adjust content and delivery method based on each learner's performance, pace, and preferences. Provides personalized learning experiences.
Mobile Learning (m-Learning): Learning method that accesses learning content through mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. Learning anytime, anywhere.
Corporate LMS: LMS designed for enterprises, supporting employee training, onboarding, compliance management, and upskilling.
Hybrid Learning Model: Learning approach that combines online learning with offline face-to-face sessions. Provides both flexibility and face-to-face interaction.
Gamification: Technique that integrates game elements such as points, badges, leaderboards, and progress tracking into learning activities to enhance learner engagement and motivation.
BYOD (Bring Your Own Device): Policy where employees bring their own devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops) to work and access corporate resources and LMS.
