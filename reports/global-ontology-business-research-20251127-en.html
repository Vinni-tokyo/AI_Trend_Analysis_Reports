Key Terminology

Ontology - A formal knowledge representation system that defines concepts, attributes, and relationships in a specific domain
Knowledge Graph - A knowledge base that represents entities and their relationships in a graph structure
GraphRAG - Technology that combines knowledge graphs with RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) to reduce LLM hallucinations
FIBO - Financial Industry Business Ontology, the standard ontology for the financial industry
BFO - Basic Formal Ontology, an ISO standard foundational ontology
FAIR - Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable data principles
