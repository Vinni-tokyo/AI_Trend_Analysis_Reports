Key Terminology

Multi-LLM Aggregator - Platform providing multiple large language models (GPT-4, Claude 3, Gemini, etc.) through a single user interface. Users can switch between models to select optimal AI. Wrtn and Poe fall into this category.
MAU (Monthly Active Users) - Monthly active user count. Wrtn achieved 5M MAU in 22 months, significantly faster growth than popular Korean app Toss (39 months).
Localization Strategy - Strategy to optimize global platforms for specific regions' languages, cultures, and needs. Wrtn specializes in Korean; Japan market entry requires Japanese quality and local needs adaptation.
Adaptive Learning - Learning system that automatically adjusts content and difficulty based on student comprehension and progress. AI analyzes in real-time to provide individually optimized learning paths.
Personalization - Customizing content and experience based on individual user characteristics, learning style, and progress. In AI education, proven to improve learning outcomes 30% and engagement 10x.
Freemium Model - Business model offering basic features free while monetizing premium features. Wrtn offers GPT-5 free for user acquisition, Poe lowered entry barriers with $5/month plan.
Bot Marketplace - Ecosystem where users create, publish, and share custom AI bots. Poe fully deployed in 2025, enabling creation of education-specialized bots (tutors, assessment support, etc.).
