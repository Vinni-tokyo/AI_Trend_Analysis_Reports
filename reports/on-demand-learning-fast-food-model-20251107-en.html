Key Terminology

Vibe Coding - A new software development methodology where AI (large language models) generates code from natural language instructions. Develop with prompt engineering skills without memorizing syntax
Microlearning - Learning units completed in short 5-10 minute sessions. Learnable on smartphones during commutes, with higher knowledge retention than traditional long lectures
Micro-credentials - Short-term skill certifications obtainable in 5-30 hours. Increasingly recognized in corporate hiring as instant credentials replacing traditional degrees (2-4 years)
AI Course Generator - Tools that automatically create complete learning courses from prompts, documents, and URLs. Represented by Coursebox, CourseAI, Synthesia
On-Demand Learning - Learning model where you learn just the skills you need, when you need them. Also called fast food-type education, instantly accessible without pre-designed curricula
Digital Badges - Visual representations of skill proofs instantly shareable online. Blockchain-verifiable and displayable on LinkedIn profiles
Learning Management System (LMS) - Platforms where companies and educational institutions manage, deliver, and assess learning content. Represented by LinkedIn Learning and SC Training
