Key Terminology

Turn-taking - Technology for naturally determining when speakers alternate in conversation. Accurate recognition of speech completion is critical
VAD (Voice Activity Detection) - Technology for detecting voice segments. Simple VAD cannot determine semantic completion of speech
Latency - Delay time from input to response. Sub-100ms enables natural conversation experience
Voice Cloning - Technology that learns a specific person's voice and reads arbitrary text in that voice
τ2-bench - Benchmark for evaluating AI reasoning ability. Measures complex task API chain execution capability
