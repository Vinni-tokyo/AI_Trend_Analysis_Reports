Key Terminology

AlphaFold - Protein structure prediction AI developed by Google DeepMind. 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry recipient.
DFT (Density Functional Theory) - Quantum mechanics-based electronic structure calculation method. Widely used in materials science and chemistry.
Foundation Model - AI model pre-trained on large-scale data that can be adapted to diverse tasks.
Generative Chemistry - Technology that uses AI to design new chemical compounds. Expanding use in drug discovery.
GraphCast - DeepMind's weather prediction AI using graph neural networks.
