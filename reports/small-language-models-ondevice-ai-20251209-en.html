Glossary

SLM (Small Language Model)
Language models with less than 10B parameters. Optimized for on-device execution.
NPU (Neural Processing Unit)
Processor specialized for AI inference. Achieves higher power efficiency than CPUs or GPUs.
INT4 Quantization
Technique compressing model weights to 4-bit integers. Reduces size by 75% while maintaining performance.
Mamba Architecture
New sequence model processing long texts with linear computation. Complements Transformer technology.
Edge AI
Executing AI inference on local devices rather than data centers.
TOPS (Tera Operations Per Second)
Metric for AI processing capability. NPUs provide tens of TOPS of dedicated compute.
