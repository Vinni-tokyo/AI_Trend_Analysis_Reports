Glossary

Synthetic Data
Artificially generated data that mimics real data's statistical properties. Used for AI training while protecting privacy.
PII (Personally Identifiable Information)
Information that can identify individuals, such as names, addresses, SSNs. Synthetic data contains no PII.
LAB (Large-scale Alignment for chatBots)
Methodology for fine-tuning LLMs using taxonomy-based synthetic data. Implemented by InstructLab.
Taxonomy
Hierarchical classification system for knowledge or skills. Used as "seeds" for synthetic data generation.
GDPR
EU General Data Protection Regulation. Imposes strict requirements on personal data handling.
Domain-Specific Model
AI model optimized for specific industries or use cases. Delivers higher accuracy than general-purpose models.
