4. Shift to Skill Measurement & Competency-Based Learning

Current Status: A common strategy among talent development unicorns is shifting from traditional "course completion tracking" to "skill measurement and mobility." Companies now prioritize which skills employees have acquired rather than what they've studied. Degreed and Go1 are investing heavily in skill visualization capabilities. Market research shows that 72% of companies prioritize candidates with micro-credentials.

Importance of Skill Measurement

‚Ä¢ Hiring Priority: 72% of companies prioritize micro-credentials
‚Ä¢ Degree-Free Hiring: IBM, Google, Microsoft eliminate degree requirements
‚Ä¢ Skill Mapping: Visualizes gaps between current and required skills
‚Ä¢ Career Mobility: Optimizes internal transfers and promotions
‚Ä¢ Data-Driven: Analyzes correlation between learning ROI and job performance

Implementation Strategy

‚Ä¢ AI Assessment: Machine learning for automatic skill level evaluation
‚Ä¢ Badge Issuance: Digital credentials to prove achievements
‚Ä¢ Recommendation Engine: AI suggests next skills to learn
‚Ä¢ Integrated Analytics: Links with HRIS and ATS data
‚Ä¢ Real-Time: Continuous skill updates and tracking

Outlook: By 2026, LXPs with skill measurement capabilities will become market standard, accelerating traditional LMS replacement. AI-powered skill assessment and recommendation engines will be key differentiators.

Key Terminology

LXP (Learning Experience Platform) - Learning experience platform beyond traditional LMS. Provides skill measurement, AI recommendations, and personalization in addition to course management
Skill Mobility - Ability for employees to move to different roles/departments within the organization using their existing skills. Requires skill visualization
Micro-Credentials - Small-scale digital certifications proving mastery of specific skills. Can be obtained faster than degrees
Collaborative Learning - Learning model where employees share and create knowledge together. Bottom-up rather than top-down
Curated Platform - Model that carefully selects and provides high-quality learning content from numerous sources. Go1 is a prime example
ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) - Annual recurring revenue. Key metric for subscription businesses
HRIS (Human Resource Information System) - Human resource information system. Examples include Workday and SAP SuccessFactors
