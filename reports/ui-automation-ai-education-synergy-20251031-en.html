Key Terminology

OmniParser: A Vision AI tool developed by Microsoft that transforms screenshots into structured UI elements. Combines YOLO and Florence-2 to automatically detect and describe buttons and icons on screen.
Playwright MCP: Stands for Model Context Protocol. A mechanism enabling LLMs (Large Language Models) to automate browser operations through Playwright. Converts natural language instructions into web actions.
WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines): Web accessibility standards established by W3C. Has levels A, AA, and AAA; educational institutions typically required to meet AA level compliance.
axe-core: An accessibility testing library developed by Deque Systems. Automatically detects WCAG violations, enabling early problem detection during development.
YuJa Panorama: An accessibility platform integrated into LMS. Automatically scores upon upload and auto-generates alternative formats (audio, Braille, ePub).
Computer Vision: Technology enabling AI to "see" and understand images and videos. In education, used for auto-grading visual tasks and reading text within images.
AI LMS: Learning Management System with embedded AI technology. Automates scheduling, grading, content delivery, and performance analysis while providing personalized learning experiences.
