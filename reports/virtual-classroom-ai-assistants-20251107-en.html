Key Terminology

AI Teaching Assistant - AI system supporting teacher tasks. Automates grading, feedback, material creation, and lesson planning, enabling teachers to focus on teaching quality improvement
AI Tutor (Virtual Tutor) - AI system providing personalized instruction to students. Uses Socratic method to encourage critical thinking, offering 24/7 accessible learning support
Engagement Analytics - Technology for real-time measurement and analysis of student attention levels, participation, and learning patterns. Implemented combining voice and facial recognition
Socratic Method - Educational technique guiding learners to discover answers through questions rather than direct instruction. Adopted by Khanmigo and Duolingo Max
Hyper-Personalization - Advanced individualized learning approach analyzing each student's learning data, comprehension, and pace to provide optimized individual learning paths
